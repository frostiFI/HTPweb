Notes to demo version of 360 webpages

This site is created to demonstrate the advantages, potential and obstacles of presenting different sites with 360 degree photographs. It's purpose is to serve as a base for learning and future projects. Some descriptions and information may need future improvement.

The demo web pages show different styles and possible issues. To accomplish that, it includes also images with questionable quality that may not be suitable for public presentation and marketing in the future.
